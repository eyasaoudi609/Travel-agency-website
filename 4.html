Ksar Ghilane

Signature Ksar Ghilane Matmata

EXCURSION PROGRAM

Departure from the hotel via the Roman road.

Coffee break at Loubigla.

Continuation towards the Ksar Ghilane oasis, surrounded by vast ochre sand dunes (Eastern Erg).

Arrival at Ksar Ghilane, free time to admire the oases around the hot spring. Swimming in the hot spring.

Possibility of camel rides or quad biking as options.

Lunch.

Departure to Matmata and visit to a troglodyte house with the locals. Enjoying a good rosemary tea and homemade bread served with olive oil and honey.

Return to Djerba.

Price per person for 5 individuals: 60 Euros.

Price per person for 4 individuals: 70 Euros.

Price per person for 3 individuals: 90 Euros.

Price per person for 2 individuals: 120 Euros.

The price includes:

- Transportation by 4x4 vehicle
- Service of a guide accredited by the ONTT
- Lunch
- Entrance fees to the visits scheduled in the program

The price does not include:

- Drinks
- Tips

Ksar Ghilane 2 Days/ 1 Night

EXCURSION PROGRAM

Day 1:

Departure from the hotel via the Roman road towards Tataouine.

Stop at Chahbania for a photo break at the salt lake.

Arrival in Tataouine, visit the market and the city center.

Continuation to Chenini, a village perched on the mountainside, for a walking tour.

Lunch in Chenini.

Departure to reach the Ksar Ghilane oasis surrounded by vast ochre sand dunes.

Possibility of swimming in the hot spring.

Departure by 4*4 along the track for a night in the Tunisian desert, to the Saharan camp.

Settlement, sunset over the desert, sandbread baking ceremony.

Dinner and overnight stay in the Tunisian desert under private tents.

Day 2:

Breakfast.

Departure to Matmata via the Berber village of Tamezret.

Lunch.

Photo stop at the Berber village of Toujane.

Return to Djerba.

Price per person for 5 individuals: 150 Euros.

Price per person for 4 individuals: 160 Euros.

Price per person for 3 individuals: 190 Euros.

Price per person for 2 individuals: 240 Euros.

The price includes:

- Transportation by 4x4 vehicle
- Service of a guide accredited by the ONTT
- Overnight stay in tents with all meals
- Entrance fees to the visits scheduled in the program

The price does not include:

- Drinks
- Tips
