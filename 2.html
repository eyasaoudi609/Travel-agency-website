Matmata-Douz

Signature Matmata Douz

EXCURSION PROGRAM

06:00 AM: Departure from the hotel by ferry. It is advisable to leave early in the morning to make the most of the day.

Technical break at the Berber village of Tamezret (Photo stop).

Continuation towards Douz, the gateway to the Sahara, a place frequented by many camel drivers.

Possibility of a camel ride as an option.

Lunch at the hotel.

In the afternoon, departure towards Matmata, renowned for its lunar landscapes, visit to a troglodyte house, and Sidi Dris, the filming location of the famous director George Lucas' film "Star Wars".

Departure to the village of Toujane (Photo stop), a village nestled in the mountains of DAHAR.

Return to Djerba.

Options:
