Tozeur

Tozeur 2 Days /1 Night

EXCURSION PROGRAM

Day 1:

Departure from your hotel towards the ferry.

Continuation towards Tozeur passing through El Hamma; en route, visit the petrified sand at "Dbebcha" and cross the largest salt lake Chott Djerid.

Arrival at the hotel and lunch.

Departure to the mountain oases: Chebika and Tamerza, located on the terrace of the Saharan Atlas, famous for their natural water sources and waterfall.

Dinner at the hotel and overnight stay.

Day 2:

Breakfast and visit to the old medina of Tozeur.

Departure towards Douz, the gateway to the desert.

Opportunity to take a camel or quad ride in the sand dunes (optional).

Departure to Matmata via the Berber village of Tamezret.

Lunch at a local's home in Tamezret.

Photo stop at the Berber village of Toujane.

Return to Djerba.

Price per person: starting from 165 Euros (Minimum 4 persons)

The price includes:

- Transportation by 4x4 vehicle
- Service of a guide accredited by the ONTT
- Accommodation with all meals included in the program.
- Entrance fees to the scheduled visits in the program
- Camel trekking

The price does not include:

- Drinks
- Tips
