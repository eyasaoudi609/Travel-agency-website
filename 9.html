4x4 Tour in Southern Tunisia

4x4 Tour in Southern Tunisia 7 Days /6 Nights

EXCURSION PROGRAM

Day 1: Arrival in Djerba

Upon arrival in Djerba, transfer to the hotel and check-in. Enjoy dinner and overnight stay in Djerba.

Day 2: Ksar Ghilane

After breakfast, depart via the Roman Road to Tataouine. Explore the Souk before heading to the Berber village of "Chenini". Lunch will be served en route. Arrive at Ksar Ghilane and settle into the camp. Enjoy an optional quad excursion (1 hour) or a camel ride to witness the sunset and visit the ancient desert fort. Dinner and overnight stay in tents.

Day 3: Discovery of Jerid

Start the day with breakfast before departing for Tozeur. Cross the Chott El Djérid salt flats and visit the ancient town of Tozeur. Lunch will be provided upon arrival. Spend the afternoon exploring the palm grove with an adventure tour. Return to the hotel and visit the Dar Chéraït Traditional Art Museum. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4: Excursion to Mountain Oases

After breakfast, embark on a journey to visit the stunning mountain oases of Chebika, Tamerza, and Midès. Return to the hotel for lunch, then venture into the OngDmel dunes to discover the Star Wars sets. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5: Matmata

Enjoy breakfast before heading to the troglodyte village of Matmata. Visit a troglodyte house and explore the famous Berber village. Dinner and overnight stay with a local host.

Day 6: Exploration of the Island

Start the day with breakfast and journey to the island of Djerba. Visit Guellela, renowned for its pottery village, and witness pottery making at a local workshop. Continue to explore the traditional arts museum and La synagogue. Then, head to Houmt Souk, the capital of Djerba, for free time to explore the traditional market. Enjoy lunch with grilled fish at a seaside restaurant. Return to the hotel for check-in and overnight stay.

Day 7: Departure

Transfer to the airport according to flight schedules.
